Introduction

Over the last several decades, an enormous amount of climate data has been collected from numerous sources. The US Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Center provides public access to this data, but the challenge is how to make sense of it. This website aims to tell a compelling story about climate change through open-source data. Our goal is to educate, motivate, and illustrate the pressing climate issues, such as rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and increasing temperatures.

Climate Change Story

CO2 Emissions in Texas

Texas has long been known for its energy-driven economy, which contributes significantly to global CO2 emissions. During the COVID-19 lockdowns, emissions dropped dramatically, showing how small changes can have a big impact.

California Coastal Emissions

California’s coastal regions face unique challenges from CO2 emissions. The ocean’s ability to absorb CO2 is decreasing, signaling a pressing environmental crisis.
